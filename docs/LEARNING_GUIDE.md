# Portuguese Learning Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Basic Greetings

- **Olá** (Hello)
- **Bom dia** (Good morning)
- **Boa tarde** (Good afternoon)
- **Boa noite** (Good evening/night)
- **Tchau** (Bye)

### 2. Essential Phrases

- **Como está?** (How are you? - formal)
- **Como vai?** (How are you? - informal)
- **Tudo bem?** (Everything good?)
- **Obrigado/a** (Thank you - m/f)
- **Por favor** (Please)

### 3. Numbers (1-10)

1. um/uma
2. dois/duas
3. três
4. quatro
5. cinco
6. seis
7. sete
8. oito
9. nove
10. dez

## Learning Tips

1. **Practice Daily** - Even 10 minutes helps
2. **Speak Aloud** - Pronunciation is key
3. **Use AI Tutor** - Ask questions freely
4. **Track Vocabulary** - Review regularly
5. **Be Patient** - Language learning takes time

## Common Mistakes

### Gendered Words
Portuguese has masculine/feminine forms:
- **o** (the - masculine): o carro (the car)
- **a** (the - feminine): a casa (the house)

### Formal vs Informal
- **Você** - You (informal, Brazilian)
- **Tu** - You (informal, European)
- **O senhor/A senhora** - You (formal)
